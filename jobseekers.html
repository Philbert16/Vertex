Finding Your Next Role

Expert tips, resources, and insights to help you land your dream tech job at Vertex.

📝

Optimise Your Profile

Keep your CV concise, highlight quantifiable achievements, and tailor it to tech roles. Use keywords that match job descriptions.

  • 2–3 pages max
  • Show impact (e.g., "reduced latency by 40%")
  • Include links to portfolio / GitHub
🔍

Browse our latest vacancies, apply for roles that match your skills, and our team will review within 48 hours.

Prepare for Interviews

Research the company, practice technical questions, and prepare thoughtful questions. We'll share interview details in advance.

  • Use STAR method for behavioural Q's
  • Practice coding problems
  • Ask about team, culture, and growth
🎯

Negotiate Confidently

Understand your market value, research salary ranges, and don't hesitate to negotiate. We'll guide you through the offer.

  • Know your baseline salary
  • Negotiate benefits + equity
  • Ask for time to decide
📚

Tech Skills to Highlight

Employers value: problem-solving, system design, cloud skills (AWS/Azure), and continuous learning. Certifications help too.

  • Certifications (AWS, GCP, etc.)
  • Open source contributions
  • Blogging / public speaking
